Best Burger Ever

Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs lean ground beef
  • 0.5 onion, finely chopped
  • 0.5 cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 oz dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 0.5 tsp crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestshire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Use hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the venter and the juices run clear, about 4-5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degress F (74 degrees C).
